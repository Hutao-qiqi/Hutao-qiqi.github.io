## Profile

Liu Shuye is an undergraduate student at the Taikang Medical School (School of Basic Medical Sciences), Wuhan University. His research interests center on single-cell omics, spatial transcriptomics, and computational biology, with a particular focus on computational identification of cell-state transitions, multimodal data integration, and formal modeling of trajectory analysis. He is actively involved in interdisciplinary projects spanning bioinformatics, medicine, and artificial intelligence.

**Framework for identifying critical transitions in single-cell trajectories**

- The work focuses on dynamic structure characterization and transition-state detection in complex cell-fate processes.
- This line of work has led to a conference presentation and ongoing manuscript preparation.
